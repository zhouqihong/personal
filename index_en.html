Biography

  • He graduated from Peking University in 2022 with a doctorate degree in education, associate professor, and master's supervisor. His research interests include artificial intelligence and language teaching, large language models, academic Chinese, computational linguistics, corpus linguistics, language acquisition, and teaching Chinese to speakers of other languages. He has presided over 1 project of the National Social Science Fund, participated in 3 national projects, 5 provincial and ministerial projects, presided over 1 humanities and social science planning project of the Chongqing Municipal Education Commission, 3 school-level projects, published more than 10 papers in "Contemporary Rhetoric", "Application of Language and Writing", "Chinese Teaching and Research" and Springer, developed 3 software, and opened courses such as "Python Basics and Data Mining", "Big Data Technology and Application", "Language Research Design and Methods" and so on. From 2009 to 2012, he taught at the Confucius Institute at the University of Botswana. Outstanding undergraduate thesis supervisor of Sichuan University of Foreign Chinese. In 2019, he was awarded the scholarship of the Chinese Academy of Chinese Language.
